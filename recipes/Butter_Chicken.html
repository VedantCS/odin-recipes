Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Set aside for 30 minutes.
  2. In a pan, melt the butter and saut√© garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  3. Add the tomato puree and cook for 10 minutes on medium heat.
  4. Add garam masala and mix well.
  5. Add the marinated chicken pieces and cook until they are done.
  6. Stir in the cream and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or naan.
